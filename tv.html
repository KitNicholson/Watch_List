Dead Boy Detectives
2024

A glorious romp of a YA‑Fantasy show. Two dead boys set themselves up as detectives, solving a chaotic collection of cases for other ghosts. It balances silliness with serious moments between it's ensemble cast members. All of whom give pitch perfect performances.

Russian Doll
2019

A mind bending fantasy driven by Natasha Lyonne, an incredible *ally*. I totally read two side characters as dating, but according to the wiki they are just friends. either way there's enough queer energy in season 1 for me to count this as a queer show.

Scavengers Regin
2023

Rarely does sci fi feel as alien as this show. It's creatures seem explicable and unnatural, and yet also perfectly embedded into their environment. It's a beautifully weird ecosystem that's always ready to pounce on the unwary.

Shout out to Levi! *Thrip*

Arcane
2021

A fantasy tale of Shakespearean proportions. Season 1 sees the cycle of violence spiraling to ever more devastating outcomes. With a vast range of fully fleshed out characters to draw from the source material, watching them hurt each other is just as painful.
