Thumper
2016

Not just a rhythm game, Thumper is rhythm violence. Every action has physicality. You don't turn into corners you slam into them. The game demands a high level of focus, flinging your space beetle through complex time signatures rarely seen in the genre.

Echo
2017

A really interesting game by a small studio with a divine sense of aesthetics. It manages to do so much within strict boundaries. With only two voiced characters, and a single destination they craft an entire strange world. Criminally overlooked, this is something special.
